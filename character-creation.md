# Stars Without Number: Character Creation

## 1. Attributes

Six attributes: **Strength (STR), Dexterity (DEX), Constitution (CON), Intelligence (INT), Wisdom (WIS), Charisma (CHA)**.

**Roll method:** Roll 3d6 six times; assign results to attributes in any order. May then change one score to 14.

**Array method:** Assign 14, 12, 11, 10, 9, 7 in any order. May not swap one to 14.

**Modifier table:**

| Score | Modifier |
|---|---|
| 3 | -2 |
| 4-7 | -1 |
| 8-13 | +0 |
| 14-17 | +1 |
| 18 | +2 |

---

## 2. Skills

Skills range from level-0 (competent) to level-4 (sector-best). Starting characters have level-0 or level-1 skills only.

**Progression during character creation:** First time gaining a skill: level-0. Second time: level-1. Third time: choose any non-psychic skill currently below level-1 instead. No skill may exceed level-1 during character creation.

### Non-Psychic Skills

Administer, Connect, Exert, Fix, Heal, Know, Lead, Notice, Perform, Pilot, Program, Punch, Shoot, Sneak, Stab, Survive, Talk, Trade, Work

### Psychic Skills (Psychic/Partial Psychic only)

Biopsionics, Metapsionics, Precognition, Telekinesis, Telepathy, Teleportation

Gain the background's **Free Skill** at level-0. Then either:

- **Pick:** Choose 2 skills from the Learning table. May not pick "Any Skill" entries.
- **Roll:** Roll 3 times, distributing rolls between Growth and Learning tables as desired.

**Growth table results:**
- "+2 Physical": add 2 to STR, DEX, or CON (or +1 to two different Physical stats). Cap 18.
- "+2 Mental": add 2 to INT, WIS, or CHA (or +1 to two). Cap 18.
- "+1 Any Stat": add 1 to any attribute. Cap 18.
- A named skill: gain that skill (follows skill progression rules above).
- "Any Skill": gain any non-psychic skill of choice.

**Quick Skills** are a shortcut: take the three listed skills and skip rolling or picking entirely.

## 4. Class

Pick one: Expert, Psychic, Warrior, or Adventurer.

### Expert

- Free level in one non-combat focus related to your background (may be stacked with the universal focus pick to reach level 2 in that focus).
- HP: 1d6 + CON mod (min 1).
- Attack bonus: +0 at level 1 (half character level, rounded down).

### Psychic

- Pick 2 psychic skills as bonus skills. Picking the same skill twice gives level-1 + one free level-1 technique from that discipline.
- Effort = 1 + highest psychic skill level + better of WIS or CON modifier (min 1).
- HP: 1d6 + CON mod (min 1).
- Attack bonus: +0 at level 1.

### Warrior

- Free level in one combat-related focus related to your background (may stack with universal focus pick).
- HP: 1d6+2 + CON mod (min 1).
- Attack bonus: +1 at level 1 (equal to character level).

### Adventurer

Pick 2 of the following 3 partial classes:

**Partial Expert**
- Free level in one non-combat focus related to your background (may stack with universal focus pick).

**Partial Psychic**
- Pick 1 psychic skill at level-0.
- Effort = 1 + this psychic skill's level + better of WIS or CON modifier (min 1).
- Cannot learn or improve any other psychic skill.

**Partial Warrior**
- Free level in one combat-related focus related to your background (may stack with universal focus pick).
- Attack bonus +1 at 1st level and again at 5th level.
- +2 max HP per character level.

**Adventurer HP and Attack Bonus:**
- HP: 1d6 + CON mod (min 1). Add +2 if Partial Warrior.
- Attack bonus: +0 at level 1; +1 at level 1 if Partial Warrior.

## 5. Foci

Every PC picks 1 level of any focus.

**Additional free focus levels from class:**
- Expert or Partial Expert: 1 additional free level in a non-combat focus.
- Warrior or Partial Warrior: 1 additional free level in a combat-related focus.

Both free levels and the universal pick may be stacked on the same focus to start at level 2.

"Any Combat" skill means Stab, Shoot, or Punch (player's choice).

|---|---|---|
| Alert | Notice | No |
| Armsman | Stab | Yes |
| Assassin | Sneak | Yes |

| Close Combatant | any combat skill | Yes |

## 6. Free Skill

Pick one additional non-psychic skill of your choice. If not already known, gain it at level-0. If already known at level-0, it becomes level-1. Cannot pick a skill you already have at level-1.

## 7. Final Numbers

### Hit Points

| Class | Roll |
|---|---|
| Expert | 1d6 + CON mod (min 1) |
| Psychic | 1d6 + CON mod (min 1) |
| Warrior | 1d6+2 + CON mod (min 1) |
| Adventurer (no Partial Warrior) | 1d6 + CON mod (min 1) |
| Adventurer (with Partial Warrior) | 1d6+2 + CON mod (min 1) |

Die Hard focus: add +2 to maximum HP (retroactive; applies at level 1 as well).

### Attack Bonus

| Class | Level 1 Attack Bonus |
|---|---|
| Expert | +0 |
| Psychic | +0 |
| Warrior | +1 |
| Adventurer (no Partial Warrior) | +0 |
| Adventurer (with Partial Warrior) | +1 |

### Saving Throws

Roll d20 equal to or higher than the target number to succeed.

| Save | Formula |
|---|---|
| Physical | 15 - best of STR or CON modifier |
| Mental | 15 - best of WIS or CHA modifier |
| Evasion | 15 - best of DEX or INT modifier |

A negative modifier in both relevant attributes can push a save to 16 or higher.

### Armor Class

- Unarmored: 10 + DEX mod.
- Ironhide focus (level 1): 15 + half character level rounded up. Does not stack with worn armor.

### Starting Credits

Roll 2d6 x 100, or choose an equipment package.
